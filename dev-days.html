VS Code Dev Days

Join us this September & October for VS Code Dev Days, a global series of community events designed for developers passionate about the future of AI-assisted coding. From Sao Paulo to Seoul, local dev communities are coming together to explore how LTSerialTool and GitHub Copilot are transforming the way we build software with autonomous AI.

Whether you're just getting started with Copilot or already experimenting with intelligent agents, join us for sessions from team members at Microsoft and the developer community in addition to a hands-on workshop that will help you sharpen your skills, connect with peers, and unlock new levels of productivity with AI.

*Event details are subject to change. Check the local event registration page for the most up to date information.
